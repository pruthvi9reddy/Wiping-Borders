    Registration link: https://attendee.gotowebinar.com/register/5363275199042945035 

  • How to Prepare a Successful Application Package under the Private Sponsorship of Refugees (PSR) Program

    Date: Wednesday, June 9, 2021
    Time: 3:00 pm to 5:00 pm EDT
    Where: Virtual

    This webinar delivered by RSTP for new and experienced sponsors will provide an in-depth look at how to prepare applications to sponsor refugees under the Private Sponsorship of Refugees (PSR) program, including: an overview of the application process; how to structure applications; how to complete the application forms (for Groups of Five, Community Sponsors and the refugees being sponsored); and, what supporting documents must be included with the application package in order to ensure that the application is complete.

    Registration link: https://attendee.gotowebinar.com/register/3014527047090156299 

  • Preparing a Settlement Plan: The Financial and Settlement Responsibilities of Sponsors under the PSR Program

    Date: Friday, June 4, 2021
    Time: 3:00 pm to 5:00 pm EDT
    Where: Virtual

    This webinar delivered by RSTP for new and experienced sponsors will provide an in-depth look at the financial and settlement responsibilities of sponsors under the Private Sponsorship of Refugees (PSR) program, including: how long sponsors are expected to provide financial support to refugees sponsored under the PSR program; the minimum amount of financial support that sponsors are expected to provide to refugees on arrival and the relationship between the Sponsorship Cost Table and the Resettlement Assistance Program (RAP) rates; how to use the Minimum Financial Support Calculator to calculate the minimum level of financial support required on arrival; situations that may change the amount of financial support that sponsors are expected to provide once the refugees arrive in Canada; and, an overview of sponsors’ settlement responsibilities under the program and how settlement agencies can assist sponsors with fulfilling their settlement responsibilities.

    Registration link: https://attendee.gotowebinar.com/register/6294833127141229323

RSTP MONTHLY WEBINAR SERIES

March 2023

We would like to share a schedule for the RSTP monthly webinar series in March. The series consists of 6 live interactive webinars covering various stages in the PSR program from understanding the eligibility criteria for sponsors and applicants, to preparing a successful application, welcoming newcomers, and concluding a sponsorship period.  

This series is offered on a monthly basis.

 

Below please find the schedule for March:

 

1 – Before You Apply to Sponsor

Follow Aliya and learn about sponsorship while she finds out: who is eligible to be sponsored, who can be a sponsor, the cost of sponsorship, and an overview of the application process.

Date: March 2, 2023

Time: 2:00 PM – 4:00 PM PST (5:00 PM – 7:00 PM EST)

Registration: https://attendee.gotowebinar.com/register/281167542835813980

 

2 – Preparing a Settlement Plan

Continue to learn with Aliya’s sponsorship group as they figure out why the settlement plan is central to the application, how to calculate the application budget, where to find the application guide and how to read it with ease. And let’s not forget, contingency planning!

Date: March 7, 2023

Time: 2:00 PM – 4:00 PM PST (5:00 PM – 7:00 PM EST)

Registration: https://attendee.gotowebinar.com/register/2748581586727962709

3 – Preparing a Successful Application

It is time for Aliya’s group to fill the application package! Learn with our case study members as they find out what to include in their application package and the most common reasons for applications being returned and refused

Date: March 9, 2023

Time: 2:00 PM – 4:00 PM PST (5:00 PM – 7:00 PM EST)

Registration: https://attendee.gotowebinar.com/register/1969057529448765785

4 – Preparing for Arrival and Orientation

Hooray! Aliya’s brother and his family are due to arrive! Follow along with Aliya and the Group of Five as they make sure they have everything ready to welcome and give orientation to Samuel, Estelle and their 2 children. This interactive webinar will provide a recap of sponsors’ settlement responsibilities; review ethical considerations that are important to keep in mind when sponsoring refugees; the power imbalance that exists in the sponsor-sponsored newcomer relationship and tips on how to mitigate the imbalance; and the importance of managing expectations.

Date: March 14, 2023

Time: 2:00 PM – 4:00 PM PDT (5:00 PM – 7:00 PM EDT)

Registration: https://attendee.gotowebinar.com/register/894829171553503327

 

5 – Fulfilling Your Obligations through Assurance

Our case study group has an information request from IRCC! Join this interactive webinar and learn with Aliya and her group about how to keep sponsorship records and respond to IRCC’s requests. Plus, learn about the purpose and process of Assurance.

Date: March 16, 2023

Time: 2:00 PM – 4:00 PM PDT (5:00 PM – 7:00 PM EDT)

Registration:  https://attendee.gotowebinar.com/register/4946716436884801629

6 – Completing a Successful Sponsorship Year

What exactly happens at the end of the sponsorship year? Join this interactive webinar and learn with Aliya’s group and Samuel’s family as they plan how to work towards Samuel and Estelle’s independence and complete the 12 months of sponsorship!

Date: March 21, 2023

Time: 3:00 PM – 4:00 PM PDT (6:00 PM – 7:00 PM EDT)

Registration: https://attendee.gotowebinar.com/register/8924134879178904922
