The post Professional Development Webinar for Settlement Professionals: Supporting Privately Sponsored Refugees appeared first on Refugee Sponsorship Training Program (RSTP).

]]> This webinar will introduce participants to the Refugee Sponsorship Training Program’s Settlement Support Connection project.

The webinar will cover the following topics:

  • Key elements of the Private Sponsorship of Refugees (PSR) and Blended Visa Office-Referred (BVOR) programs
  • IRCC’s expectations for sponsored refugees to access government-funded settlement services
  • The complementary roles of sponsors and settlement professionals in providing settlement support
  • Description of the Settlement Support Connection project,
  • Best practices for collaboration with sponsors.

Settlement professionals who attend this webinar may receive referrals from our team.
