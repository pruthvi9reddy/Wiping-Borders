Ask me Anything about Group of Five/Community Sponsorship

April 27 @ 5:00 pm - 6:30 pm

  • « Calculations Matter! A Virtual Workshop for Groups of Five
  • Building Bridges: An Introduction to the Private Sponsorship of Refugees Program »

During this webinar, we encourage all participants to take advantage of the opportunity to ask questions about the program. Our team can answer your queries if you’re curious about the eligibility criteria or have questions about financial and settlement responsibilities. We understand that navigating the application process can be daunting, so we want to ensure that all participants understand the program and its requirements. Our goal is to equip you with the knowledge and resources necessary to make informed decisions regarding your participation in the program. So don’t hesitate to ask any general questions that you may have – our team is here to help!
