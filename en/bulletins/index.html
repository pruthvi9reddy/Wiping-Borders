RSTP Bulletins

The RSTP bulletin is a bi-weekly newsletter featuring recent policy and program updates on Canada’s refugee sponsorship programs, global forced migration news, upcoming events and webinars, available refugee profiles in the Joint Assistance Sponsorship (JAS) program and other information relating to the Private Sponsorship of Refugees (PSR) program. The bulletin is shared electronically with SAHs through the SAH listserv and with other sponsoring groups via the PSR listserv.
